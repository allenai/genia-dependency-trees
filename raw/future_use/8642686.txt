The Epstein-Barr virus-encoded nuclear antigen EBNA-5 accumulates in PML-containing bodies.
EBNA-5 is one of the Epstein-Barr virus (EBV)-encoded nuclear proteins required for immortalization of human B lymphocytes.
In the nuclei of EBV-transformed lymphoblastoid cell lines EBNA-5 is preferentially targetted to distinct nuclear foci.
